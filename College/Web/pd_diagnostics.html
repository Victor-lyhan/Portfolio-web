Continuation after last year

After building the rehabilitation glove, I realized its reach was limited to those who already had access to the device. To make an impact earlier and on a larger scale, I shifted toward software—creating AI diagnostics that only require a simple video or voice recording. Early diagnosis is far more critical than late-stage rehabilitation, and by removing hardware barriers, I could make Parkinson’s detection more accessible, affordable, and scalable worldwide.
